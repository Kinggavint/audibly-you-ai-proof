How export works

One profile. Many shapes.

You write your profile once inside Audibly. When you need it elsewhere, pick the tool, pick the format, and copy or download. We handle the per-tool formatting in the background so you don’t have to think about it.

  • One-click copy for the most common AI tools
  • Auto-refresh option so exports stay current
  • Versioned exports so you can roll back changes
