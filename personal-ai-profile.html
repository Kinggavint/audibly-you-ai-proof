Personal AI profile

A living profile that captures who you are at work.

Your goals, your tools, your style, your unfinished projects — in one place that AI can actually read.

What it learns

Signals that actually matter, never the rest.

Audibly You AI focuses on the few things that change every AI conversation: how you talk, what you’re building, what tone you want, and where you keep getting stuck.

  • Working role, goals, and current projects
  • Voice, tone, and writing preferences
  • Recurring topics and unfinished threads
  • Preferred AI tools, formats, and workflows
  • Learning style and experience level by topic
What it doesn’t learn

Hard limits, by design.

Some categories are excluded from the profile by default. We don’t store them, infer them, or guess at them.

  • No silent reading of your inbox, calendar, or messages
  • No payment data, ID numbers, or sensitive personal records
  • No source it ingests without your explicit approval
  • No third-party advertising profiles — ever
