About Audibly You AI

We’re building the missing layer between you and every AI tool you use.

AI is everywhere now — but it forgets you the second you switch tabs. Audibly You AI exists to fix that. We help your technology understand the person behind the prompts.

Mission

Personal AI should belong to people, not platforms.

Every major AI assistant is racing to know you better. That’s good for the assistant. It’s not always good for you. Your context, preferences, and history shouldn’t be trapped inside one company’s product.

Audibly You AI is a portable personal profile. You build it once. You take it with you. You decide who gets to read it.

What we believe

Four principles guiding every product decision.

People come before models

Models change every quarter. People don’t. The profile is built around you, not whichever AI is on top this week.

Approval is the default

Nothing is learned without you approving the source. Every input has an off switch you can find.

Open before locked-in

Your profile exports cleanly. Markdown, JSON, plain text. No proprietary trap.

Plain language wins

If a privacy control needs a glossary, we wrote it wrong. Everything we ship gets edited until a normal human can use it.

Founder

Built by people who use AI all day.
