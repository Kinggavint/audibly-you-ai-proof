Privacy and security

Your profile, your sources, your call.

Audibly You AI is built so that you only have data learned from sources you approve. You can review it, edit it, export it, or delete it at any time.

Our approach

Five plain-language commitments.

1. Approval, not assumption

We don’t learn from anything until you explicitly approve the source. No silent reading, no inferred ingestion, no surprises.

2. You own your profile

Your profile belongs to you. Export it in open formats. Move it. Delete it. We don’t hold it hostage and we don’t resell it.

3. Encryption in transit and at rest

All profile data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is scoped to your account and audited internally.

4. No advertising data sales

We don’t sell your profile to advertisers, data brokers, or third-party AI companies. Period.

5. Delete means delete

One click removes your profile from active systems. Backups are purged on a documented schedule, not kept forever.

6. Plain-language controls

Every privacy setting is written in normal English, not legalese. If you can’t find the off switch, that’s a bug we fix.

What we collect

Only what you approve, plus the basics.

  • Sources you explicitly connect (chat exports, uploads, integrations)
  • Profile content you write or accept
  • Account essentials (email, login, billing if applicable)
  • Standard product analytics so we can fix bugs
What we don’t

Off-limits, by default.

  • No silent ingestion of your inbox, calendar, or messages
  • No payment data, ID numbers, or sensitive personal records
  • No selling or sharing your profile with advertisers
  • No training third-party AI models on your private profile
Your controls

Everything you need to stay in charge.

Source toggles

Turn any source on or off in one click. Disconnected sources stop contributing immediately.

Line-by-line edits

Rewrite or delete any single line of your profile.

Export anytime

Markdown, JSON, plain text. Your data leaves with you.

Account deletion

Delete your profile and account from a single page. We confirm in writing.

Have a question we haven’t answered? Contact us — we’ll write back in plain English.
