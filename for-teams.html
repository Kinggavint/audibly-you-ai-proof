For teams

Give every employee a personal AI profile that actually fits their role.

Generic AI training rolls off most teams in a week. Personal profiles stick because they’re built around the work each person actually does.

The team angle

Personalized AI, at company scale.

Audibly You AI for Teams gives every employee their own profile, tuned to their role, the AI tools your company has approved, and the goals their manager cares about. The result: faster onboarding to AI, less hand-holding, and far better answers from every assistant.

  • Profiles per employee, owned by the employee
  • Optional company guardrails on tone and approved tools
  • Centralized billing, decentralized control
Use cases

Where teams see the biggest lift.

AI onboarding

New hires get a starter profile and a personalized AI course on day one instead of a 60-minute video they’ll forget.

Role-specific training

Sales, ops, marketing, support — each team gets AI courses built around the tools and tasks they actually use.

Productivity uplift

Profiles cut the “explain yourself again” tax employees pay every time they open a new AI tool.

Knowledge continuity

When someone leaves, the team-approved parts of their profile transfer cleanly to whoever picks up the work.

Tool standardization

Roll out new AI tools faster — existing profiles get auto-formatted to fit the new platform.

Compliance-friendly

Approve which sources can feed company profiles. Audit who connected what.

Built for IT and people leaders

Decentralized profiles, centralized oversight.

Admin controls

Set company-wide defaults for approved AI tools, allowed export formats, and source types. Employees still own and edit their own profiles.

SSO & user provisioning

Roadmap support for major SSO providers and SCIM provisioning for fast rollouts. Talk to us about your stack.

Reporting

Aggregate, anonymous reporting on profile adoption and course completion. Never personal content.

Privacy by default

Each employee’s profile is theirs. Admins see counts and policies, never private profile contents.
