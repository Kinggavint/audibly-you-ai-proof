Personalized AI courses

Learn AI in a way that matches your goals, style, and experience.

Your profile becomes a curriculum. Audibly turns what it knows about you into focused mini-courses that actually fit how you work.

The concept

Generic AI courses aren’t built for you. These are.

Most AI courses assume you’re starting from zero or already a developer. Most people are neither. Audibly reads your profile and writes a course around what you actually do, what you already know, and what you said you want next.

  • Tuned to your role and the AI tools you use
  • Adjusted for your stated experience level
  • Paced to match how you said you like to learn
  • Refreshed as your profile and goals change
Sample plan

“Cut my research time in half”

A 4-lesson course tuned to a consultant who already uses ChatGPT and Perplexity daily.

  • Lesson 1 — Better source-pinned research with Perplexity
  • Lesson 2 — ChatGPT Custom Instructions that earn their keep
