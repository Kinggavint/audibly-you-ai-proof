How it works

Four steps from blank slate to a profile that travels with you.

No setup marathon. No data scraping in the background. You drive every step.

1

Tell us who you are

A short intake covers your role, goals, the AI tools you use, and how you like to learn. Two minutes, no quiz vibes. You can skip anything.

  • Role and main projects
  • Preferred tone and writing style
  • AI tools you already use
2

Approve the sources it can learn from

Connect chat history exports, upload reference docs, or just talk to it. You decide which sources count and you can revoke any of them later.

  • ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity history exports
  • PDF, DOCX, MD, TXT uploads
  • Direct chat with Audibly to add context
3

Review and edit your profile

Audibly drafts a structured profile and shows it back to you in plain language. Keep what’s right. Edit what isn’t. Delete anything you don’t want.

  • Section-by-section review
  • One-click edits and deletes
  • “Why is this here?” trace on every line
4

Export and use it everywhere

Generate a profile snippet for ChatGPT, a system prompt for Claude, a JSON file for your own scripts — whatever you need. Update it as your life updates.

  • Per-tool exports for major AI assistants
  • Markdown, JSON, plain-text downloads
  • Auto-refreshing profile that learns over time
Behind the scenes

What Audibly is doing while you work.

Pattern reading

It studies the way you ask questions, the topics you return to, and the kind of answers that work for you.

Profile drafting

It writes and rewrites your profile in clean Markdown so any AI tool can read it instantly.

Course suggestions

It spots gaps you’d benefit from closing and proposes short, focused AI courses tailored to you.
