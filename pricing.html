Pricing FAQ

Common pricing questions.

Five things people ask most often about Audibly You AI plans.

How much does Audibly You AI cost?

Final pricing is being finalized with our beta users and will be published before public launch. The structure is a free tier, an individual plan in the $10–$20 per month range, and a teams plan starting at roughly $15 per seat per month.

Will there be a free plan?

Yes. The free tier lets you start a personal AI profile, run basic exports to ChatGPT and Claude, and take an introductory course at no cost. Always free, no credit card required.

What’s included in the Individual plan?

The Individual plan unlocks the full export catalog (every supported AI tool), profile versioning so you can roll back changes, deeper personalized courses, and priority support. Final feature list publishes at launch.

How does the Teams plan work?

The Teams plan is per-seat with admin controls, role-based access, usage reporting, and SSO. Built for companies rolling AI out across multiple roles where each person needs their own portable profile.

Can I get early access before pricing is published?

Yes. Join the early-access list or book a demo. Early-access users get founding-member pricing locked in for 12 months when public pricing goes live.
